About cyclone freddy

It all started somewhere in the Indian ocean next to Australian waters. Surprisingly, no one way injured during the origination. The cyclone made its ways into the shores of Africa is it hovered over Madagascar. It hit Mozambique hard, it repelled it force, ignited its turbulence via the heatwave that killed billions of fish off the shores of Madagascar then headed straight for Malawi.
   
After causing devastation in Malawi, the cyclone set back on the same trajectory it took to arrive in Africa. Without deviating from its line of travel. Not only is this weird movement too conspicuous to be called natural. It clearly indicates the possibility of external man made forces controlling the weather.

Until more evidence show, I can't be certain if this is truly foul play, until then, we accept defeat and mercy to the all mighty to spare the lives of most of us for the few. It wasn't even about having infrastructure or wealth. If you were unlucky, you and your assets all get swept away.
